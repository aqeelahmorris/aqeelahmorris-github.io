About Me

Hello! My name is Aqeelah, and I'm a developing graphic designer and enthusiastic
student eager to dive into the exciting realm of visual communication. I have a true
passion for converting ideas into appealing images and am now pursuing degrees in
Interactive Visual Communication and Branding. My design career began with a desire
to see my ideas come to life, and since then, I've been on a quest to study and experiment
with various design approaches. While I'm still in the beginning phases of my graphic design
journey,I'm already delving into Specific Aspects of Design, such as illustration and layout
design. Each assignment allows me to not only use what I've learned in the classroom, but also
to stretch my creative muscles. I'm looking forward to expanding my horizons.
